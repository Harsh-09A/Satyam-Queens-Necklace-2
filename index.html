overview

Elevate Your Lifestyle in the Heart of Luxury

Satyam Queens Necklace is the highly anticipated project in Upper Kharghar, offering extraordinary living at an affordable price. The project features luxurious high-rise towers with four levels of parking, a stunning podium with world-class amenities on the 5th floor, and Jodi flats available for those seeking spacious living. With environmental clearance and building permissions already secured, RERA approval is expected soon. If you're dreaming of a home in Kharghar, this remarkable development is worth a visit.

Drawing inspiration from the iconic Queen's Necklace at Marine Drive, renowned for its crescent-shaped promenade and shimmering city lights resembling a necklace, this project brings a touch of elegance and timeless charm to Navi Mumbai. Located in Upper Kharghar, the development recreates this grandeur with a unique, contemporary residential experience. Highlights include a sprawling 30,000 sq. ft. recreation zone and a breathtaking terrace at 265 feet above ground. Don't miss the chance to be part of this extraordinary living experience.
